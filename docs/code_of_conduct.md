# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to providing a welcoming and inclusive environment for everyone.

Participation in this project should be a harassment-free experience regardless of:

- Experience level
- Gender identity
- Sexual orientation
- Disability
- Ethnicity
- Age
- Nationality
- Religion

---

## Project Maintainers Message

This is not the United States of America.  Your 'Freedom of Speech' does not trump other participants to freedom of safety and security.

If you can't accept this then this project is not going to be a very welcoming place for you.

---

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive
- Assume good intent
- Provide helpful feedback
- Focus on improving the project

---

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment or discrimination
- Personal attacks
- Trolling
- Deliberate disruption of discussions

---

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, close issues, or block contributors who violate this code of conduct.

---

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please contact the project maintainer privately.

---

This Code of Conduct applies within project spaces and public interactions related to this project.
